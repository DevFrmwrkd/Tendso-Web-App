FILE / ABOUT

Three mechanics, one yard, no upsells.

Cedar Auto Works was started in 2009 in a leaking arch under the Sheaf Valley railway line. The roof's been fixed twice since. The same two mechanics who opened it are still under your bonnet — and we added a third when we got our second MOT bay in 2018.

We work on everything petrol and diesel, hybrids included, most light commercials, and any classic that you can get up our ramp. We don't do bodywork — we'll send you to someone who does it properly.

We're paid by the hour, not by the part. That's the most important sentence on this page.

FILE / FAQ

Questions we hear
at the workshop door.

If yours isn't here, phone the workshop on 0114 305 8821 or send a message. We try to reply within an hour during open time.

Do you take walk-ins?

For tyre and battery work and visual checks, yes. For service, MOT, and diagnostics please book — we run a tight schedule and don't want you waiting in the yard.

How long does an MOT take?

Around 45 minutes if booked. If you wait, we put the kettle on; if you'd rather come back, we'll text you when it's done.

Will my manufacturer warranty be affected?

No — under the Block Exemption Regulation, independent garages can service new vehicles without voiding the manufacturer warranty, provided we stamp the book and use approved parts. We do both.

Can you collect my car?

Yes, free inside S1–S11. We'll bring it back the same evening or the next morning, whichever suits.

Do you work on EVs?

Standard service work, brakes, suspension, tyres, 12V battery, cabin systems — yes. High-voltage battery diagnostics — we send out to a partner who has the certification.

Will you give me a courtesy car?

We have two, free for any job over four hours. Booked first-come — let us know when you book.

Do you accept fleet / company accounts?

Yes. Monthly invoicing for accounts over six months. Drop us a line.

What if something goes wrong after I collect?

Phone us. Bring it back. Twelve-month parts and labour warranty on everything we fit, no exceptions and no small print.
