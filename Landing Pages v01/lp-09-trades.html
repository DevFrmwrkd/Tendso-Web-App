FILE / ABOUT

A three-van team, on call every day of the year.

Truelock started as one van in Roath in 2012 and has grown to three engineers and a part-time dispatcher who happens to also be the founder's mum. We cover the whole of Cardiff and the Vale, every domestic system from a leaking radiator to a complete plant-room.

Two of us are Gas Safe Level 3, both with unvented hot-water tickets and ACS commercial gas. The newest engineer finishes her commercial board in February. We're WaterSafe-approved and Worcester-Bosch-accredited.

We don't have a shop. We have a workshop in Caerphilly where we stock parts, a dispatcher who knows what's on each van, and a habit of texting you on the way.

FILE / FAQ

Questions
before you call.

If yours isn't here, ring the dispatcher on 029 2055 8740 or message any time. Emergencies always to the 24-hour line.

What counts as an emergency?

Burst pipes, no heat or hot water in winter, leaks coming through ceilings, smell of gas, or a blocked drain backing up. Anything you'd ring 999 for plumbing-side is what we pick up at 3am.

Do you charge a call-out fee?

No call-out fee if we're providing a quote. For diagnostic work the first hour is fixed; you decide whether to proceed after that.

How quickly can you come out?

Same day across Cardiff and the Vale for genuine emergencies. Most non-emergency jobs are scheduled within 48 hours.

Do you work for letting agents and landlords?

Yes — block rates for portfolios of five-plus properties, monthly invoicing, digital CP12 certificates delivered to the agent and the tenant.

Are you VAT-registered?

Yes — VAT № GB 213 7421 09. All invoices include the VAT number; we can issue zero-VAT invoices on qualifying disabled-facility works.

What manufacturers do you fit?

Worcester-Bosch (accredited), Vaillant, Ideal, Baxi, Glow-worm. We'll fit what suits the house, not what makes us the biggest commission.

Will you give a like-for-like quote?

For boiler swaps yes — we'll match a competitor quote on identical specification with the same warranty terms. We won't match a lower spec to win the job.

Are you insured?

£5m public liability, £10m employers' liability. Certificates of insurance available on request before any work.
