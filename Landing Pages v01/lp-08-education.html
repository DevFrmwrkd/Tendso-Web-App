About the school

A small school that has been here a long time.

Solferino opened on Cork Street in 2003, in a Georgian house that had previously been a piano-restoration workshop and, before that, a sweet factory. It still smells faintly of resin and barley sugar.

Six teachers, three pianos, a small concert room, and a slightly bigger waiting room. We teach individual lessons, small theory groups, and an open chamber session on Wednesday evenings that any pupil can drop in on once they're past Grade 3.

We're examiner-affiliated with the RIAM and the ABRSM — but we are not a grade factory. Pupils who want to follow the grade path can; pupils who want to learn songs by their grandmother can too.

Questions

Before you
enrol.

If yours isn't here, ring the office on +353 1 554 3082.

How young can children start?

Suzuki-method violin from age four; piano from age five; voice from age seven. We meet every prospective pupil for a free intro session before we begin.

Do you only teach classical music?

Not at all. Half of our piano pupils are learning contemporary, jazz or song accompaniment. We follow the pupil, not the curriculum.

Do we have to do exam grades?

Only if you want to. Around 60% of pupils take grade exams; the rest learn for the pleasure of it and play in our term recitals.

What instruments do you have on site?

Three pianos, two violins (loan), two cellos (loan), three classical guitars (loan). Anything else, you bring your own.

Can adults enrol?

Yes — about a third of our pupils are adults, half of whom are absolute beginners. We have a dedicated adult-beginners pathway.

How does the term work?

Three terms a year, matching school terms. Each term is 11 lessons. We bill at the start of each term.

What's the cancellation policy?

We need a term's notice to stop. Inside a term, missed lessons are reimbursed if we can fit a make-up; otherwise they're forfeit.

Is the building accessible?

We're in a Georgian house — one step at the front door, ramp available on request. One ground-floor teaching room.
