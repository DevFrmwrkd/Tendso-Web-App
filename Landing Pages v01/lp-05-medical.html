About the practice

A small practice that takes its time.

Brightwater opened on Charlotte Street in 2008 and has grown to four clinicians and two hygienists. We share three things — a refusal to upsell cosmetic work nobody asked for, a commitment to long appointments for nervous patients, and an early-morning list for people who'd rather see us at seven than at noon.

Our principal dentist trained at King's College London and holds an MJDF and a postgraduate diploma in implantology. Our hygiene team is led by a clinician with twenty-two years in periodontal care. We're CQC-rated 'Good' on every domain in our most recent inspection.

We treat children from the age of one. We treat patients with dental phobia using a stepped-exposure programme written with a clinical psychologist. We don't sell whitening at the front desk.

Why patients stay

Questions

Things we're asked at the front desk.

If yours isn't here, phone reception on 01225 309 244 or email us.

Are you taking new NHS patients?

Adult NHS lists open and close depending on capacity. NHS children remain open at all times. Phone reception or check our website's banner — we update both as soon as anything changes.

What does a new-patient exam cost?

Private new-patient exams are charged at a flat fee, listed on our published price list. NHS exams are charged at the standard NHS band 1 charge set by central government. Both are explained before you book.

Do you sedate anxious patients?

We offer non-sedative anxiety pathways in-house and refer to a partner clinic for IV sedation when it's clinically appropriate. We never push sedation as a first step.

How urgent is urgent?

Same-day if you call before 10am with significant pain, swelling or trauma. Out of hours, NHS 111 is our triage; for registered private patients there is a dedicated emergency phone number.

Do you treat children?

Yes — from the age of one for familiarisation visits and from two for full examinations. NHS children's care is available throughout.

Are you wheelchair accessible?

Yes — step-free entry, an accessible WC on the ground floor, and one treatment room large enough for a wheelchair transfer with hoist.

Do you offer interest-free finance?

We work with a regulated finance partner for treatments over £500. Subject to status; full information at consultation.
