Good to know

Questions, answered.

Still unsure? Message us — a real person on Valencia St will reply.

What weight is the cotton?+
Our tees are a heavyweight 240gsm organic combed cotton; oxfords are a midweight 140gsm washed cotton. Both stay opaque and hold their shape.
How do your shirts fit?+
We offer classic and boxy fits. Every product page lists full measurements, or book a flagship fitting and we'll size you in person.
Do they shrink?+
Everything is pre-shrunk and garment-washed, so what you try on is what you keep. Wash cold, hang or tumble low.
What's your return policy?+
Free 30-day returns on unworn shirts, plus free repairs on seams and buttons for the life of the shirt.
Do you do custom or team orders?+
Yes — add a monogram online, or talk to us about small-run embroidery for teams, events, and gifts.
