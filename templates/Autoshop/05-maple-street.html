Our Story

A neighborhood shop, run on trust

We opened Apex with one rule: explain everything, fix only what's broken, and stand behind the work. Every car gets photographed during inspection, so you see what we see before approving a thing.

Our techs train on the same diagnostic platforms the dealerships use — we just answer the phone faster and charge you less. Fifteen years on, most of our work still comes from neighbors telling neighbors.

Questions

Good to know

01Do you work on my make and model?+

Domestic, Asian, and most European makes — including hybrids and turbocharged engines. If we can't help, we'll point you to someone who can.

02Will service void my factory warranty?+

No. Independent shops can perform scheduled maintenance without affecting your manufacturer warranty, and we document everything.

03Do I get an estimate first?+

Always. You'll get a written, photo-backed estimate and we won't start work until you approve it.

04How long are most repairs?+

Many services are same-day. For anything longer we offer free loaner cars so you're never stranded.

05What does the warranty cover?+

3 years or 36,000 miles on parts and labor, honored nationwide through our service network.
