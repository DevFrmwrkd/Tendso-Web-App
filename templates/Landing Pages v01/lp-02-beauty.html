The studio

Slow hair, by appointment.

Atelier Noir opened on Tib Street in 2017 with one chair, one kettle, and a colour shelf that fit on a single trolley. We've grown to four stylists and a colour technician, but the kettle is the same.

Every guest is booked into a two-hour window. That isn't because every visit takes two hours — it's because we don't want to ever rush you out. The room is quiet on purpose.

Our work is published in Vogue Italia, British Vogue, Dazed, and a stack of pamphlets the photographer's mother prints every Christmas.

Questions

Before you book.

We've answered the ones we hear most. If yours isn't here, ring the studio on 0161 482 7330.

Do I need a patch test?

Yes, for any colour service if you haven't visited us before, or if it's been over six months. Patch tests are done in person, 48 hours before your appointment.

How far in advance do you book?

Cuts are usually two to three weeks out. Colour and correction work is four to six weeks. We hold a small number of last-minute slots — call to ask.

Do you cut all hair types?

Yes — every stylist trains across textures, including coily and Type 4 hair. Tell us your texture when you enquire and we'll match you to the right person.

What's your cancellation policy?

Free up to 24 hours before your appointment. Inside 24 hours we charge 50% of the booked service to the card on file.

Is the studio accessible?

Step-free entry, accessible WC on the ground floor, two chairs with adjustable height. Please mention specific needs when you book.

Do you sell products?

A small, curated shelf — only what we use on guests. We don't take retail commissions.

Can I bring my child / dog?

Children under 12 only if they're being seen. Dogs welcome at the front of the studio.
