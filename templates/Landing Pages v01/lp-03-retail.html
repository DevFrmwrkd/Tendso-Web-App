FILE 02 · ABOUT THE STORE

A small store that fixes things.

Maple & Iron opened on NE Alberta in 1978 with a single till, a key machine, and a paint mixer Dad bought from a closing-down sale in Aloha. Two of those three are still here.

We're the second generation now — and the first generation still comes in on Saturdays, mostly to argue about how we've rearranged the screw bins. The store is one room and a workshop in the back. If we don't have it we'll tell you where to get it.

We don't carry everything. We carry the right thing. And if you bring in a broken hinge or a snapped pull cord, we'll often have a workable answer for you in five minutes.

FILE 08 · FAQ

Things people ask at the door.

If your question isn't here, call the counter on (503) 555-0184 or stop in. Someone is always at the till during open hours.

Do you take phone orders?

We'll happily pull and hold an item for you. Call the counter, give us 45 minutes, and we'll have it bagged at the till. Card on file for pickups over $50.

Do you carry plumbing parts?

Common residential — yes. Compression fittings, P-traps, supply lines, wax rings, drain snakes. Commercial sizes we'll order.

How does tool rental work?

By the day or by the weekend. Photo ID and a card on file. Pick up after 9, return by 5. Weekend rentals leave Friday at 4 and come back Monday at 10.

Do you sharpen serrated knives?

Yes, including offset and Wüsthof bread knives. Drop-off Monday through Wednesday, pickup Friday after 2.

Do you match paint from a chip?

Most of the time. Bring in a chip the size of a dime or bigger; we'll spectrometer-match and mix the smallest amount that gets you safely through your job.

Do you sell propane refills?

Yes — 20lb and 30lb tanks. We refill, not exchange, so you keep your tank.

Are you the same family that ran the store in the 80s?

Yes. Second generation now, with the first still showing up on Saturdays.
