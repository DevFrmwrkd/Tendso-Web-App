FILE / ABOUT

A gym, not a fitness brand.

Forge opened above the Marston Print Works in 2016 with three platforms, two squat racks, and a kettle that has since been replaced by a slightly nicer kettle.

Five thousand square feet, no televisions, no mirrors, and a sound system loud enough to know we mean it.

We coach the powerlifts, the Olympic lifts, and the everyday strength work that holds your back together at 50. Programmes are written by a person, for a person.

Members range from 19 to 71. We don't do challenges. We don't take photos at the door. We don't sell supplements.

FILE / WHY

Four things we don't move on.

01

Coached, not class'd.

Every coached session has a max of six people. The coach knows your name, your numbers, and your back.

02

ASCC-accredited staff.

Every coach holds the UKSCA's accreditation. Two are BASES S&C registrants.

03

Equipment that lasts.

Eleiko bars, Sorinex racks, calibrated competition plates from 0.5 to 25 kg.

04

Two-week free trial.

Use everything. Take a coached session. Walk away if it doesn't suit.

FILE / FAQ

Before
you sign up.

If yours isn't here, ring 0113 220 8801.

Do I need to be strong already?

No. Half of new members have never picked up a barbell. The six-week Foundations programme is built for that exact starting point.

What does the free trial include?

Two weeks of full open-gym access plus one coached intro session. No card needed to start.

Is there parking?

Two reserved bays at the back; pay-and-display on Wellington Street. Four-minute walk from Leeds station.

Can I bring a guest?

Members get one free guest pass a month. Drop-ins welcome at the standard day rate.

Do you have showers?

Two changing rooms, three showers, lockers. Towels for hire if you forget.

Is it busy?

Peaks 6–9 and 5:30–8. Off-peak is genuinely off-peak — sometimes empty.

Do you do contracts?

Monthly rolling membership. Cancel any time with one calendar month's notice.
