Good to know

Questions, answered.

How does wash & fold pricing work?+
Wash & fold is priced by the pound, weighed when it arrives. There's a small minimum per order, and we'll always confirm the weight before we start. No hidden surcharges.
How fast is the turnaround?+
Most wash & fold orders are ready the next day, and early drop-offs can often be same-day. We'll text you the moment your laundry is clean, folded, and ready to go.
Is pickup and delivery really free?+
Yes — pickup and delivery are free within our Seattle service area on qualifying orders. Just schedule a window online and leave your bag out; we handle the rest.
Can I use the machines myself?+
Absolutely. Our self-service laundromat is open daily with 30+ clean, modern washers and dryers, including big-capacity machines for comforters and bedding. Attendants are on hand if you need a hand.
