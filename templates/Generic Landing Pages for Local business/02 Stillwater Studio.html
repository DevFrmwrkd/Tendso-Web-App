Our story

A quiet room on a loud street.

Stillwater began as a single borrowed room and a standing intention: make somewhere people could put the day down for an hour. No mirrors to perform for, no leaderboards, no rush.

We kept classes small on purpose. Teachers adjust by hand, remember your injuries, and meet you where your body is that morning — beginner, returning, or deep in your practice.

Move gently, breathe fully.

Good to know

Questions, answered.

I'm a total beginner — is that okay?+
More than okay. Most of our classes are mixed-level with modifications offered throughout, and new students get a gentle intro flow. Tell the teacher it's your first time and they'll keep an eye out.
What should I bring?+
Just comfortable clothes and yourself. We have mats, props, and blankets here, and grippy socks available for reformer classes. Water fountain's by the door.
Do I need a membership to start?+
No. Drop in for a single class or grab a class pass first. If you fall in love with the place, memberships are month-to-month with no contract.
Can you work around an injury?+
Yes — let us know when you book or arrive. Our teachers are trained to modify, and for anything more specific, a private session lets us build movement entirely around what you need.
