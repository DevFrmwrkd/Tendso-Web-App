faq

Common questions.

How fast do you respond?+
Our average response time is under 15 minutes during business hours, with critical issues escalated immediately. Managed clients also get 24/7 monitoring that often fixes things before a ticket is even opened.
How does pricing work?+
Managed plans are a flat monthly rate per user or device, so your IT cost is predictable. We'll scope a plan during your free review — no long lock-in contracts and no surprise charges per call.
Do you only work remotely?+
Most issues we solve remotely in minutes, but we're an Austin-based team and come on-site whenever a problem needs hands-on work — new offices, hardware, cabling, or anything physical.
What size businesses do you support?+
We specialize in small and mid-size businesses, typically from a handful of seats up to around 100. If you're outgrowing break-fix IT, that's exactly where we fit.
