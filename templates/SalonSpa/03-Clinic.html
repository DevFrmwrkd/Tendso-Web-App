about lumé

One unhurried place for hair, skin, and body.

Lumé began with one idea — a single, calm space where the lighting is soft, the products are clean, and the people remember your name. Our licensed stylists, estheticians, and massage therapists work out of six private rooms, blending exacting technique with genuine, unhurried care.

good to know

Questions, answered

Q.01Do you take walk-ins?+

We love walk-ins when chairs are open, but booking online guarantees your time and your stylist.

Q.02Are your products clean and low-tox?+

Yes — ammonia-free color and low-tox, fragrance-considerate lines throughout the house.

Q.03Can I book hair and spa in one visit?+

Absolutely. Tell us when you book and we'll coordinate back-to-back rooms so it's seamless.

Q.04Do you offer consultations?+

Every visit starts with a quick, no-pressure consult. Color corrections get a dedicated one first.

Q.05What about parking?+

Street and garage parking are a short walk away in the Pearl; we'll point you to the closest.

Q.06What's your cancellation policy?+

Life happens — just give us 24 hours' notice so we can offer the spot to someone else.
