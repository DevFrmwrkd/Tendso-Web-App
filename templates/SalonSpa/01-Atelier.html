The house

Lumé began with one idea — a single, unhurried place for hair, skin, and body, where the lighting is soft and the people remember your name.

Our team of licensed stylists, estheticians, and massage therapists works out of six private rooms in the heart of the Pearl District. We blend exacting technique with genuine, unhurried care — and we keep our product lines clean, low-tox, and fragrance-considerate, so the calm starts the moment you walk in.

Good to know

Questions, answered

Do you take walk-ins?+

We love walk-ins when chairs are open, but booking online guarantees your time and your stylist.

Are your products clean and low-tox?+

Yes — ammonia-free color and low-tox, fragrance-considerate lines throughout the house.

Can I book hair and spa in one visit?+

Absolutely. Tell us when you book and we'll coordinate back-to-back rooms so it's seamless.

Do you offer consultations?+

Every visit starts with a quick, no-pressure consult. Color corrections get a dedicated one first.

What about parking?+

Street and garage parking are a short walk away in the Pearl; we'll point you to the closest on arrival.

What's your cancellation policy?+

Life happens — just give us 24 hours' notice so we can offer the spot to someone else.
